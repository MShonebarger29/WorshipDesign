What are Ways Some Ways I Can Worship Jesus

What are Ways I Can Worship Jesus?

  • SingingThere are lots of Christian music out there. You can find some and listen to it throughout your day. Examples of Christian music: All Hail King Jesus, Grattitude, Awake my soul, Back to Life, ext.
  • Reading Your BibleReading your Bible is a really good way of getting to know God and His voice. We can learn to tell what He says for us to do and what He says not to do.
  • Talents God has given each of us our own special talents, that makes us diffrent. Not everyone has the same exact talents. We should use our talents to glorify God.
  • PrayerPrayer is talking to God. We shoudl talk to God daily, thanking Him, praying for others, and talking to Him about our life. Yes, God knows everything, but He still wants us to talk to Him.

As we gather to worship Jesus, let us do so with grateful hearts, open minds, and a deep sense of reverence for the One who gave His life for us.
